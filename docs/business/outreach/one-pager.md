## Frequently asked

**"How do you compare to Transit App / Moovit?"**
They're aggregators optimized for big cities. We give you a branded operator-owned channel with push alerts you control. Different value, often complementary.

**"What about our existing fare collection vendor?"**
We integrate via deep-link (Token Transit, Masabi, etc.) or stay out of fare entirely. We don't replace fare systems.

**"What if you go out of business?"**
Source code escrow + 14-day data export clause in every contract. You can pull GTFS + brand and walk away to another vendor.

**"How long is the contract?"**
12-month initial term, then month-to-month (or auto-renew annual). Either party can exit with 60 days' notice.

**"GDPR / CCPA compliance?"**
Standard data processor agreement included. We don't sell passenger data, ever. Sub-processors: Apple, Google, Vercel, Hetzner, GitHub. Full list on request.

**"Can you handle public agency procurement?"**
Below most direct-procurement thresholds ($299/month = $3,588/year, well under typical $25k competitive bid line). Happy to provide W-9 or international equivalents for vendor onboarding.
